THE ELECTIONS EXPLAINED:

The University runs elections so you can chose who should be the next group of students to run and lead your Socities and Union. There are lots of positions you could nominate yourself for incluuding full-time paid positions and part-time voluntary positions. Elected students then set the direction of the Student Union and Societies to improve students' University experience.
